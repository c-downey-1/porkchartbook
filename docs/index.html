High Frequency Data

Daily estimated pork production, million lb (daily hogs × carcass weight).

Daily Hogs Processed

Daily hogs processed, thousand head (AMS LM_HG201, covered packers).

Barrow & Gilt Prices

$/cwt, weighted average base and net prices.

Primal Values

$/cwt for loin, butt, picnic, rib, ham, and belly.

Pork Cutout Value

$/cwt, daily composite cutout.

$/cwt spread proxy.

Herd & Forward Supply

Quarterly Hogs & Pigs indicators for inventory, market-weight mix, farrowings, productivity, and pig crop.

Pig Crop

Million head, monthly where available.

Breeding Stock Inventory

Million head, quarterly.

Thousand head per month (federally inspected) — breeding-herd cull signal.

Pigs per litter.

Sows Farrowed

Million head.
III

Slaughter & Production

Monthly USDA NASS commercial slaughter, pork production, average live and dressed weights, and frozen cold-storage stocks (~99% industry coverage).

Commercial Hog Slaughter

Million head per month, stacked — federally inspected plus the non-FI remainder totals commercial slaughter.

Commercial Pork Production

Million lb per month, carcass weight, all commercial plants.

Monthly Production Comparison

Million lb, commercial pork production by month — a line per year (last 10), with 2026 (yellow) and 2025 (orange) highlighted against prior years.

Average Slaughter Weight

Pounds per head — dressed (carcass) weight stacked inside average live weight.

Cold Storage

Million lb, frozen pork stocks and major cuts.
IV

Retail & Domestic Demand

Retail feature activity, advertised pork prices, and consumer price proxies.

Retail Feature Activity

Feature rate and activity index from AMS retail pork reports.

Featured Retail Pork Price

$/lb, weighted featured price with FRED bacon proxy where available.

Latest Featured Items

Highest-store-count advertised pork items in the latest AMS retail report.
V

Trade

Monthly pork trade flows, destination mix, monthly export comparison, and exports as a share of production.

Pork Exports: US vs. Brazil

Million lb per month. US exports are ERS carcass weight; Brazil exports (Comex Stat) are product weight, so treat the comparison as directional.

Top Export Destinations

Million lb per month, stacked by destination with an Other category totaling all pork exports.

Top Import Sources

Million lb per month, stacked by source with an Other category totaling all US pork imports.

Monthly Export Comparison

Million lb, US pork exports by calendar month with each year side by side — year-over-year and seasonal comparison.

Exports as Share of Production

US pork exports divided by NASS commercial pork production (carcass weight), 12-month rolling average.

Brazil Top Export Destinations

Million lb per month, stacked by destination with an Other category totaling all Brazil fresh/frozen pork exports.

Corn and soybean meal proxy index from public FRED series.

Monthly Cutout-Net Spread

$/cwt, monthly average cutout less net hog price.

Risk Watch

Public-source issues that should stay on the monthly page.
